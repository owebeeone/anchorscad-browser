Datatree Injected Fields for Torus

A torus, also provides ability to speficy a sector of the cross section.

This includes a cage that is always the full torus in which the torus is
a complete circular extrusion. The anchors cage_centre_start, cage_centre_end
and cage_surface are on the cage and are stable despite the sector chosen.

Args:
metadata_fn: Number of segements for arcs and splines for openscad shapes.
angle: The sweep angle to extrude.
convexity: Openscad convexity parameter.
use_polyhedrons: Use polyhedrons instead of rotate_extrude.
Other args:
r_hole, r_section, section_start_angle, section_sweep_angle, path_fn, fn, fa, fs, hide_cage
